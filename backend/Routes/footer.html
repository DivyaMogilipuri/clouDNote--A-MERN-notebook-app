Helping Hands is a student-volunteer organization committedfor the welfare of student community in RGUKT-Nuzvid, a A.P state government university which caters to the educational needs of rural youth of Andhra Pradesh. Founded in the year 2009 by a group of enthusiastic students, it has been a driving force in instilling a sense of leadership and social responsibility among students who are otherwise mostly from the very modest/poor families.
